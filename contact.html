Frequently Asked Questions

What do I do with a found item?

You can either bring it to the Lakeside High School office or keep it until contacted by staff to return the item to its owner.

What if no one claims the item?

Unclaimed items are held at the lost and found for a reasonable period; however, if nobody claims them, they may be donated to the A.C.E. closet.

Can I report that I lost an item?

This site is to locate lost items that have been reported by students and staff. If you are missing an item and think it may be at the lost and found, check out the Items page.

What info should I include in my report?

Include the item name, a detailed description (color, size, brand, or any identifying features), where you found it, and a photo if you are able to. The more information you can give, the easier it becomes for the owner to find it.
